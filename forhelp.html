step 1: seek help

sometimes, recognizing and admitting you aren't well is the hardest step.
since you are here, good job!

step 2: finding an outlet

yes, things don't always go too well.
to cope with life, it is vital that you find an outlet.
don't bottle the emotions in.
let it out.
finding an outlet that suits your requirements and habits can solve most of your problems -
whether it is in the form of music, sports or writing it out in the form of poetry-
l e t i t a l l o u t .
talk to your family, your friends, your peers, your co-workers.

step 3: self care

sometimes we forget to take care of ourselves.
here's a tip: don't forget.
remember to love yourself first.
a positive mindset can take you a long way.
it might be hard when you start, but with practice, a positive mind really helps you boost your serotonin.
some not so conventional tips might be to -
  • take a break from social media.
    social media depression has become a casual term these days. please, try taking a break.
  • do not, i repeat- do not listen to sad songs.
    the songs you listen to influence your mood. don't try to bring yourself down with depressing songs.
  • surround yourself with happy people.
    i know it's a bitter pill to swallow, but if you land yourself in a depressing environment, with unhappy people, the odds are, you aren't gonna end up being any happier than your peers.
    stay away from those toxic people.

step 4: keep track of your progress

keep track of what you are doing. ask yourself the questions -
  • how did you feel today?
  • did you try to remain positive today?
  • how well do you think you coped today?
  • if you didn't do so well today, what do you think catalysed your unhappiness?

some general tips...

  • this might be a cliche- but do not isolate yourself.
    there's always people out there who care for you. talk to them.
    you are not doing them or yourself any favours by shutting them out.
  • keep yourself busy.
    don't dwell on things for too long. free time = thinking time, and that isn't a great idea when you are not in a good place.
  • refrain from self harming practices.
    it doesn't help.
    mentally or physically torturing yourself- b a d .
    repeat this as a mantra in your head: you don't deserve the pain. you deserve happiness. you desereve love. from the people around you. from yourself. never forget that.
  • if nothing helps- it is strongly recommended to get some prefessional help. therapists- they know what they are doing.

apart from all this-
just know that you are not alone.

things will get better.
hold on for a bit longer- you are strong enough :)
